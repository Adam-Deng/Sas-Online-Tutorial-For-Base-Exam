Viewing and Storing Charts

As you create charts, you might want to manage them. For example, you can

  • view individual graphs by scrolling in the GRAPH window
  • view individual graphs by selecting them in the Results window
  • specify a temporary or permanent output catalog in which to store your graphs
  • view and manage entries that are stored in catalogs.

If you have completed the lesson , you might want to skip this topic and go on to Additional Features.
