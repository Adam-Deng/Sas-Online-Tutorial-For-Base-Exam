Table of Contents and Index

In selected courses, you can use the expandable table of contents and index to select a specific lesson or topic. Just click Contents & Index on the home page.
 

  • In the Contents tab, courses contain lessons and each lesson contains various topics. You can expand a module and lesson and then click a topic title to open that topic.


  • In the Index tab, you can type terms to locate topics of interest and then click Display to open the related topic in the main training window.




The table of contents and index are available only from selected courses.
